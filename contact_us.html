FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

What are the school hours?
School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Where is the school located?
The school is located at 123 Main Street, City, State, ZIP code.

How can I contact the school?
You can reach the school by phone at (123) 456-7890 or by email at info@schoolname.edu.

How do I enroll my child at the school?
You can enroll your child by completing the online application form available on our website or by visiting the school office.

What documents are required for enrollment?
Required documents include the child's birth certificate, immunization records, proof of residency, and previous school records.

Is there a tuition fee, and how much is it?
Yes, there is a tuition fee. The amount varies by grade level and payment plans are available. Please contact the admissions office for detailed information.

What curriculum does the school follow?
The school follows the [specific curriculum, e.g., Common Core Standards, IB Curriculum, etc.]. Detailed information about our curriculum is available on our website.

What extracurricular activities are offered?
We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, and various clubs. You can find a full list on our Extracurricular Activities page.

How often are parent-teacher conferences held?
Parent-teacher conferences are held twice a year, in the fall and spring. Additional meetings can be scheduled upon request.

What should I do if my child will be absent?
Please notify the school office by phone or email as soon as possible if your child will be absent. A written note is also required upon the child's return.

What is the school's policy on tardiness?
Students are expected to arrive on time. Three tardies will be equivalent to one unexcused absence.

How can I request a leave of absence for my child?
To request a leave of absence, please submit a written request to the principal at least two weeks in advance.

What are the school's health and safety protocols?
The school follows strict health and safety protocols including regular sanitization, social distancing, and mandatory mask-wearing where applicable.

What should I do if my child has a medical condition?
Please inform the school nurse and provide any necessary documentation from your child's doctor.
